Company Handbook — General Policies

REMOTE WORK
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Remote work requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least one
week in advance. Employees are expected to be available during core hours
(10am–4pm local time) regardless of work location. Equipment provided for
remote work includes a company laptop and a one-time $200 home office
stipend. IT support tickets for remote equipment issues should be filed
under the "Remote-IT" category.

PAID TIME OFF
Full-time employees accrue 18 days of PTO per year, credited monthly.
Unused PTO up to 5 days carries over into the next calendar year. PTO
requests should be submitted at least 3 business days in advance through
the HR portal.

EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
Business expenses under $50 can be submitted without pre-approval. Expenses
above $50 require manager sign-off before the purchase. Reimbursement
requests must include an itemized receipt and be filed within 30 days.

For any HR questions, contact the internal HR team at
hr@example-corp.internal.
