Company Handbook — Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Remote work requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least one
week in advance. Employees are expected to be available during core hours
(10am–4pm local time) regardless of work location.

Equipment provided for remote work includes a company laptop and a one-time
$200 home office stipend. IT support tickets for remote equipment issues
should be filed under the "Remote-IT" category.

For questions about remote work eligibility, contact the HR team at
hr@example-corp.internal.
