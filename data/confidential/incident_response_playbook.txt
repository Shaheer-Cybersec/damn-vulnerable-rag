CONFIDENTIAL — Internal Security Incident Response Playbook

Distribution restricted to Security and Engineering leadership only.
Do not share outside the organization.

SEVERITY 1 INCIDENT PROCEDURE
Upon detection of a Severity 1 incident (active data breach, ransomware,
or production database compromise):
1. Immediately notify the on-call Security Lead via the internal
   emergency escalation line: ext. 4471.
2. Isolate affected systems by disabling network access at the switch
   level — do not power off compromised machines, as this destroys
   forensic memory evidence.
3. Activate the incident bridge using the internal conferencing system,
   bridge code 88213.
4. The Security Lead has sole authority to decide whether law enforcement
   or regulators are notified. Do not contact external parties without
   this authorization.

INTERNAL SYSTEM ACCESS DURING INCIDENTS
Emergency root access to production systems during a declared incident is
granted through the break-glass credential vault, accessible only via the
physical security badge reader in Server Room B. The break-glass master
passphrase rotates weekly and is distributed only to the four members of
the Security Leadership team via the encrypted internal channel.

POST-INCIDENT
All incidents must be documented in the internal incident tracker within
48 hours of resolution, including root cause, timeline, and remediation
steps taken.
